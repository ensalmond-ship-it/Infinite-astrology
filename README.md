# Infinite Astrology Hub 🌟

An all-in-one astrology platform designed for **accessibility** and **ease of use**.

## What is This?

Infinite Astrology Hub helps you:
- ✨ Get your birth chart reading
- 📍 Find the best places to live based on your astrology
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Learn what's good for your family and children
- 📖 Read daily horoscopes and guidance
- 💬 Access everything with text-to-speech and easy mode

## Accessibility First

This app is designed for everyone, especially people with dyslexia:
- 🔊 **Text-to-Speech** - Click any text to hear it read aloud
- 🎨 **Easy Mode** - Simpler layout and language
- 🔤 **Large Text** - Easy to read fonts
- 🌙 **Dark/Light Mode** - Easier on the eyes
